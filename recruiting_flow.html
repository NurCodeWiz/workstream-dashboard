Recruiting Flow

1
Application
Candidates apply through the website or LinkedIn. Website applicants are automatically added to Airtable, and LinkedIn candidates are added daily via a Claude script. All applicants are also added to the LuckyHiring app. This way, all candidates are collected in Airtable.
2
AI Scoring
Based on the role they applied for, an AI prompt automatically runs and assigns each candidate a score.
Owner
Nur
3
Zoom Screening Decision
Using these scores, we decide whether a candidate should be invited to a Zoom screening.
Owner
Nur
4
Zoom Screening
We run Zoom screenings with selected candidates and fill out the role-specific score sheet.
Owner
Nur
Didn't perform well
Upload recording to Airtable → add feedback → mark as rejected
Performed well
Complete score table → move forward
5
Manager Review
Share the candidate and their evaluation with the hiring manager for that role.
Owner
Nur
6
Manager Decision
Based on the manager's feedback, the candidate is either invited to a technical interview or rejected.
Owner
YanHarrison
For AI/ML Researcher role: candidates who pass the phone screen and are liked by the manager are given a take-home task.
7
Technical Interview → 1:1 with Devrim
Candidates who pass the technical interview are invited to a 1:1 with Devrim.
Owner
YanHarrison
8
Offer
Based on the final decision, an offer is sent.
Owner
DevrimArtia
